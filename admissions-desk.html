    What the demo includes

    One workflow, two audiences.

    The public page helps students and parents get clear guidance immediately. The internal side helps CL Global respond faster without losing the human admissions voice.

    01

    Website advisor

    Embedded beside the course pages so students can ask which OxCam programme fits their background, age, interests, and dates.

    02

    AI matching

    Compares each enquiry with AI+, System Design & Engineering Thinking+, OxCam Research+, and custom course pathways.

    03

    Lead queue

    Captures name, intent, urgency, missing details, and recommended next step for admissions or partnership follow-up.

    04

    Reply approval

    Drafts a warm response in CL Global's tone while keeping staff in control before anything is sent.

    High-value use cases

    Built around real enquiries.

    The strongest hook is not "we made a website." It is "we made the admissions conversation easier to win."

    Course-fit questions

    Parents and students can ask which course to choose and receive a confident, catalogue-aware answer.

    • Best for AI+, engineering, research, and age-band questions.
    • Works as a public website widget.
    • Passes hot leads to staff immediately.

    Parent trust questions

    The assistant can answer safety, college life, supervision, English level, and Cambridge experience questions.

    • Uses public programme information and approved answers.
    • Escalates sensitive questions to the team.
    • Reduces repetitive back-and-forth.

    Partner proposals

    School and institution enquiries can become structured partnership follow-ups instead of generic contact replies.

    • Identifies bespoke course opportunities.
    • Drafts proposal questions for the owner.
    • Prioritizes higher-ticket conversations.
    How it would run

    AI where customers ask. Codex where the business works.

    The public advisor can answer in real time. Codex can support the owner behind the scenes by reviewing new enquiries, improving drafts, and keeping the workflow healthy.

    Real time

    Website visitors get course-fit guidance while they are still interested.

    Every 30 min

    New Gmail and form enquiries can be grouped, ranked, and turned into approval-ready drafts.

    Owner control

    Staff approve, edit, or request a stronger reply before messages go out.

    Ongoing

    The system learns from approved replies, new brochures, programme dates, and updated admissions policies.
