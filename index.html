An innovative approach

Innovative, inspiring & impactful

Every year, CL Global Academy welcomes over 1,500 talented students to Cambridge, where they engage in intellectually challenging and collaborative courses. Our programmes are taught by professors and instructors affiliated with the University of Cambridge, who nurture interdisciplinary curiosity and diverse academic interests through an innovative, project-based learning approach.

Authentic Cambridge Experiences

Where tradition meets innovation.

During OxCam Programmes, students live, dine and study in central Cambridge colleges, benefiting from long-standing partnerships and an academic environment shaped by discovery, debate and collaboration.

College life Formal dinners, college settings and time in the city create an immersive Cambridge rhythm.
Cultural depth Punting, museums, walking tours and local activities connect study with place.
Global cohort Students learn alongside ambitious peers from diverse backgrounds and academic interests.
