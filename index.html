Our approach

A Cambridge programme with real academic shape.

CL Global Academy welcomes more than 1,500 students to Cambridge each year for intellectually demanding, collaborative courses. Cambridge experience

Study, live and connect in the city.

Students spend time in central Cambridge settings, balancing serious academic work with cultural experiences that make the programme feel rooted in place.

College life Formal dinners, college settings and time in the city create an immersive Cambridge rhythm.
Cultural depth Punting, museums, walking tours and local activities connect study with place.
Global cohort Students learn alongside ambitious peers from diverse backgrounds and academic interests.

Academic standard & support

Serious teaching, human guidance.

The programmes pair academically ambitious instruction with Fellow Advisors who help students navigate seminars, project work, college life and the everyday texture of Cambridge.

Expert instruction Professors, researchers and industry experts help students explore advanced subject matter.
Fellow Advisors Current Cambridge undergraduates offer practical, friendly support across the programme.
Recognised quality The academic experience is presented with clear standards, structure and accountability.
Confident outcomes Students leave with stronger ideas, clearer ambitions and a wider international network.
